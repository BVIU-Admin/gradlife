Gradlife.in - Service Discontinuation

Dear users of Gradlife.in,

It is with deep regret that we announce the discontinuation of services on Gradlife.in, the popular fresher job searching site. This decision has been made due to the significant increase in maintenance costs required to sustain our operations.

Since our inception, our primary goal has been to connect fresh graduates with job opportunities and support them in their career journeys. We have taken pride in assisting numerous individuals in finding employment and kickstarting their professional lives.

Unfortunately, the rising expenses associated with running Gradlife.in have become unsustainable for us. As a result, we have had to make the difficult choice to cease our services on this platform.

We want to express our heartfelt gratitude to all our users for their trust, support, and engagement throughout the years. It has been an incredible journey, and we are honored to have played a part in helping young talents pursue their dreams.

While Gradlife.in will no longer be available, we encourage you to explore other reputable job portals and career resources to continue your job search and stay informed about the latest opportunities in your field.

If you have any questions or require assistance, please feel free to reach out to us at info@gradlife.in. We will do our best to address any concerns you may have.

Once again, we extend our heartfelt appreciation for being a part of the Gradlife.in community and wish you all the best in your future endeavors.

Sincerely,

The Gradlife.in Team
